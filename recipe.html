Chocolate cake

"This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. Thisversion of the recipe isfast and quickand your kids will love it."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate:200g

Butter: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and butter togather

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
